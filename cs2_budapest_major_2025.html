1. Player Rating Leaderboard
CTE · RANK() · AVG · JOIN
Ranks all players by average HLTV Rating 3.0 across all maps played, with K/D and ADR context. Uses a CTE + window function to assign rank.
-- Full-event player rating leaderboard
WITH agg AS (
  SELECT
    p.player_name,
    p.team,
    p.country,
    COUNT(pm.map_id)                                    AS maps_played,
    ROUND(AVG(pm.hltv_rating), 2)                        AS avg_rating,
    ROUND(SUM(pm.kills) * 1.0 / NULLIF(SUM(pm.deaths),0), 2) AS kd_ratio,
    ROUND(AVG(pm.adr), 1)                              AS avg_adr,
    ROUND(AVG(pm.kast_pct), 1)                         AS avg_kast
  FROM players p
  JOIN player_map_stats pm ON p.player_id = pm.player_id
  JOIN maps m              ON pm.map_id = m.map_id
  WHERE m.stage = 'playoffs'
  GROUP BY p.player_name, p.team, p.country
  HAVING COUNT(pm.map_id) >= 3  -- min 3 maps for meaningful avg
)
SELECT
  RANK() OVER (ORDER BY avg_rating DESC) AS rank,
  player_name, team, maps_played,
  avg_rating, kd_ratio, avg_adr, avg_kast
FROM agg
ORDER BY avg_rating DESC
LIMIT 20;
2. Map CT vs T Win Rate Analysis
CASE WHEN · GROUP BY · RATIO
Calculates CT-side and T-side win rates per map across all playoff matches. Reveals map balance and strategic tendencies.
-- CT vs T win rate per map in playoffs
SELECT
  m.map_name,
  COUNT(r.round_id)                                         AS total_rounds,

  COUNT(CASE WHEN r.winning_side = 'CT' THEN 1 END)         AS ct_wins,
  COUNT(CASE WHEN r.winning_side = 'T'  THEN 1 END)         AS t_wins,

  ROUND(
    COUNT(CASE WHEN r.winning_side = 'CT' THEN 1 END)
    * 100.0 / NULLIF(COUNT(r.round_id), 0)
  , 1)                                                       AS ct_win_pct,

  ROUND(
    COUNT(CASE WHEN r.winning_side = 'T' THEN 1 END)
    * 100.0 / NULLIF(COUNT(r.round_id), 0)
  , 1)                                                       AS t_win_pct

FROM rounds r
JOIN maps m ON r.map_id = m.map_id
WHERE m.stage = 'playoffs'
  AND r.round_type != 'overtime'  -- regulation rounds only
GROUP BY m.map_name
ORDER BY ct_win_pct DESC;
3. Economy Round Win Rate
CTE · CASE WHEN · NTILE
Classifies each round by team economy type (Full Buy / Force / Eco / Pistol) and calculates win rate per category — a key CS2 analytical technique.
-- Economy type classification and win rates
WITH eco_classified AS (
  SELECT
    r.match_id,
    r.round_num,
    r.team,
    r.round_win,
    r.team_spend,

    CASE
      WHEN r.round_num IN (1, 16)    THEN 'Pistol'
      WHEN r.team_spend < 2000        THEN 'Eco'
      WHEN r.team_spend < 4000        THEN 'Force Buy'
      ELSE                              'Full Buy'
    END                                   AS buy_type

  FROM rounds r
  JOIN maps m ON r.map_id = m.map_id
  WHERE m.match_id IN ('GRAND_FINAL')
    AND r.team = 'Vitality'
)
SELECT
  buy_type,
  COUNT(*)                                         AS rounds_played,
  SUM(round_win)                                   AS rounds_won,
  ROUND(SUM(round_win) * 100.0 / COUNT(*), 1)   AS win_rate_pct,
  ROUND(AVG(team_spend), 0)                       AS avg_spend
FROM eco_classified
GROUP BY buy_type
ORDER BY
  CASE buy_type
    WHEN 'Pistol'   THEN 1
    WHEN 'Full Buy' THEN 2
    WHEN 'Force Buy'THEN 3
    WHEN 'Eco'      THEN 4
  END;
4. Player Peak Map Performance
WINDOW · ROW_NUMBER · SELF JOIN
Finds each player's single best map rating at the event — used to identify clutch performers and highlight peak moments like ropz's 2.89 on Overpass.
-- Peak single-map rating per player (career-best finder)
WITH ranked_maps AS (
  SELECT
    p.player_name,
    p.team,
    m.map_name,
    m.match_description,
    pm.hltv_rating,
    pm.kills,
    pm.deaths,
    pm.adr,
    ROW_NUMBER() OVER (
      PARTITION BY p.player_id
      ORDER BY pm.hltv_rating DESC
    )                                  AS rn
  FROM player_map_stats pm
  JOIN players p ON pm.player_id  = p.player_id
  JOIN maps m    ON pm.map_id     = m.map_id
)
SELECT
  player_name,
  team,
  map_name,
  match_description,
  hltv_rating   AS peak_rating,
  kills,
  deaths,
  ROUND(kills * 1.0 / NULLIF(deaths,0), 2) AS kd,
  adr
FROM ranked_maps
WHERE rn = 1
ORDER BY peak_rating DESC
LIMIT 15;

Data-driven findings from the StarLadder Budapest Major 2025 — presented as stakeholder-ready analytical conclusions.

FINDING 01
Vitality — First Back-to-Back Champions Since Astralis 2019
3 Majors
Vitality won Budapest 2025 and Austin 2025 in the same calendar year, becoming the first team to win consecutive Majors in 6 years. Their 2025 season included 9 trophy wins — matching Astralis's legendary single-season record.
FINDING 02
donk — Highest Individual Rating Despite Early Exit
1.57 rating
Team Spirit's donk finished as the highest-rated player at the entire event with a 1.57 rating and +82 K-D differential across 9 maps — yet Spirit were eliminated in the semifinals. Seven of his nine maps ended above a 1.40 rating.
FINDING 03
ZywOo — Historic 3rd Major MVP. Most in CS History
1.38 rating
ZywOo became the first player ever to win three Major MVPs, with his KPRW (kills per round win) of 1.17 being the decisive tiebreaker over ropz (1.02) and mezii (0.92) in what was the closest MVP race in Major history.
FINDING 04
Nuke CT Side: 62% Win Rate — Most CT-Sided Map
62% CT rate
Nuke had a dominant CT-side win rate of 62% across all playoff matches, the highest of any map in the pool. This played directly into FaZe's only Grand Final map win, with karrigan posting a 2.90 CT rating and 158 ADR on the map.
FINDING 05
Vitality 3-Player Top-8 Ratings — Unprecedented Depth
ZywOo / ropz / mezii
Vitality placed three players inside the top 8 rated players of the event — ZywOo (1.38), ropz (1.37), and mezii. This team-rating depth is arguably more impressive than any single individual stat, reflecting true systemic dominance.
FINDING 06
ropz — Career-Best Map Rating Ever Recorded
2.89 rating
Robin "ropz" Kool posted a 2.89 rating on Overpass in Map 4 of the Grand Final — his highest-ever single-map rating in his career. He recorded a 22-7 K-D, 144.1 ADR, and +18.78 Round Swing as Vitality sealed the title 13-2.
